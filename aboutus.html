INTRODUCTION

Since 2017, We provide best education for our valuable students.

The Aditya Private ITI is a Industrial Training institute (ITI) that provides training to understand industry and market. ITI is a Technical course one can join after successful completion of class 10th or Matriculation. ITI is a great course for students who want to do a professional technical course in the short term.

The Aditya Pvt. ITI is established under Raunak Educational Trust in 2016 by our founder Shri Pawan Kumar. It was a dream of our founder to open an ITI where professionals come to Upgrade their knowledge and expertise, Share best practices about latest industry trends, and Brainstorm. ITI offers end-to-end training solutions that are short –term, intensive and specially designed to build expertise among professionals. Our commitment to you: quality training from experienced faculty, flexible pathway selection to suit individual needs, progress feedback after each block of training, regular monitoring by the management, a small and friendly institute that maintains all work ethics required by the industry, and a local, convenient location.
