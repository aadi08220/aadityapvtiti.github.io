Welcome To ADITYA PVT. ITI

The ADITYA Private ITI is a Industrial Training institute (ITI) that provides training to understand industry and market. ITI is a Technical course one can join after successful completion of class 10th or Matriculation. ITI is a great course for students who want to do a professional technical course in the short term.

The ADITYA Pvt. ITI is established under Raunak Educational Trust in 2016 by our founder Shri Pawan Kumar. It was a dream of our founder to open an ITI where professionals come to Upgrade their knowledge and expertise, Share best practices about latest industry trends, and Brainstorm. ITI offers end-to-end training solutions that are short –term, intensive and specially designed to build expertise among professionals. Our commitment to you: quality training from experienced faculty, flexible pathway selection to suit individual needs, progress feedback after each block of training, regular monitoring by the management, a small and friendly institute that maintains all work ethics required by the industry, and a local, convenient location.

Electrician Trade

Electrician trade under CTS- Craftsman Training Scheme is one of the most popular courses. ITI electrician course is a short term professional technical course, which enables students to work on different types of electrical wiring and equipment. This course provides students with fundamental knowledge of the necessary electrical fields. Under this course, students are given a good knowledge of Electrical equipment, Wiring, safety in the electrical field, and other technical details. You are given Practical and Theoretical Knowledge of Electrical Field.

Fitter Trade

Fitter is a 2-year ITI course approved with the NCVT. The Fitter trade is related to the Mechanical branch. In this course, the candidates can get the knowledge about fittings like Pipe fittings, Machine fitting, and structure fittings are the main working areas, on which a fitter works. You are given Practical and Theoretical Knowledge of Fitter Field. After the completion of which, the candidates will have scope in various government and private organizations along with a handsome salary package.
